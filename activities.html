Our Programmes

At PEYATIM, we believe every child deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential. Our programmes nurture the mind, body, and soul of every child in our care.

🏫

Formal Education Support

All children at PEYATIM attend government schools nearby. We provide full financial support for school fees, uniforms, textbooks, stationery, and exam fees from primary through secondary level. For outstanding students, scholarships are available for tertiary education at local universities and colleges. In 2025, 93% of our SPM candidates achieved at least 5As.

📚

Quranic Education & Tahfiz Programme

Islamic education is the heart of PEYATIM. Our full-time Tahfiz Al-Quran Centre, established in 2003, offers structured memorisation classes guided by experienced huffaz. Children follow a structured syllabus to complete the 30 juz over three to five years. To date, 47 children from PEYATIM have become certified hafiz/hafizah.

🔧

Vocational & Life Skills Training

Our Skills Training Centre, opened in 2010, equips older children (aged 14+) with practical vocational skills for independent adult life. Current offerings include:

  • Computer literacy and basic IT skills
  • Culinary arts and food handling
  • Sewing, tailoring, and fashion design
  • Basic automotive maintenance
  • Entrepreneurship and financial literacy
⚽

Sports & Recreation

Physical activity and healthy recreation are essential for every child's development. PEYATIM encourages participation in a range of sports including:

  • Futsal — weekly inter-branch league
  • Badminton — PEYATIM Inter-Branch Tournament (annual)
  • Taekwondo — certified instructor, white to black belt pathway
  • Swimming — basic water safety and competitive training
  • Camping and outdoor adventure programmes
🤝

Community Service & Outreach

PEYATIM instils a culture of giving back. Our residents regularly participate in gotong-royong (community clean-ups), elderly home visits, hospital visits, and charitable food distribution during Ramadan. These activities build empathy, social responsibility, and a sense of community belonging — values that last a lifetime.
