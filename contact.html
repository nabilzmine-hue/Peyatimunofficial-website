General Enquiries

🤝 Volunteer With Us

We welcome passionate volunteers — educators, medical professionals, counsellors, and tradespeople. Email volunteer@peyatim.org.my with your name, skills, and availability. Volunteer orientation is held every quarter.

🏢 Corporate Partnership

Fulfil your CSR obligations through PEYATIM. Contact us at csr@peyatim.org.my for partnership packages including event sponsorship, adopt-a-child programmes, and in-kind contribution drives.

🕒 Response Time: All email enquiries are responded to within 3 working days. For urgent matters, please call +603-2148 1234.
