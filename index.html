Welcome to PEYATIM

Pertubuhan Kebajikan Anak-Anak Yatim Islam Malaysia (PEYATIM) is a registered non-profit welfare organisation under the Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat (JKM) Malaysia. Founded in 1982, we have been the home and family for hundreds of orphaned and underprivileged Muslim children throughout Malaysia.

Every child in our care receives a warm bed, nutritious meals, formal schooling, Quranic education, and the emotional support needed to grow into confident, productive members of society.

Your generosity — through zakat, sadaqah, or volunteering — directly transforms the life of a child in need.
