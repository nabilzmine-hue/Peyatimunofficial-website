How to Donate

Bank Transfer • Zakat & Sadaqah • Online Donation • Sponsor a Child

Why Your Donation Matters

Your generosity provides more than material support — it gives a child hope, security, and the chance to dream of a better future.

PEYATIM operates entirely through the goodwill of donors like you. All contributions go directly toward the welfare of our children. We maintain a strict policy of financial transparency, with annual audited accounts published and available to any donor upon request.

As a registered welfare organisation under Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat (JKM), your donations to PEYATIM are eligible for income tax exemption under Section 44(6) of the Income Tax Act 1967.

100% of your zakat and sadaqah contributions are channelled directly to our children's education, welfare, and development programmes. Our administrative costs are covered by separate corporate sponsorships.

Other Donation Methods

✉ Cheque

Make cheques payable to “PERTUBUHAN KEBAJIKAN ANAK-ANAK YATIM ISLAM MALAYSIA” and mail to our headquarters:

PEYATIM Headquarters — Finance & Donation Unit
No. 64, Jalan Maharajalela, 50480 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
Please write your name, contact number, and purpose of donation on the back of the cheque.

📱 Online & FPX Payment

PEYATIM accepts online donations via FPX (Financial Process Exchange). Visit the official PEYATIM website at www.peyatim.org.my to access the secure online donation portal.

📦 In-Kind Donations

We gratefully accept the following in-kind donations. Drop off at HQ (Mon–Fri, 8:30am – 5:00pm) or arrange for collection:

  • Non-perishable food items (rice, cooking oil, flour, canned goods)
  • Children's clothing (all ages, clean and in good condition)
  • School bags, stationery, and textbooks
  • Computers, tablets, and educational software
  • Furniture, mattresses, and household items
  • Sports equipment and recreational items
